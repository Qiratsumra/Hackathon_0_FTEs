## Data Collection Process

### Step 1: Gather Raw Data
- Extract revenue data from Accounting/ folder
- Count completed tasks from Done/ folder
- Analyze metrics from Logs/ folder
- Review goals from Business_Goals.md
- Collect system status from Dashboard.md

### Step 2: Calculate Metrics
- Compute weekly revenue totals
- Calculate completion rates
- Determine trend directions
- Assess performance against targets

### Step 3: Identify Patterns
- Spot anomalies in performance
- Recognize recurring bottlenecks
- Identify successful strategies
- Note emerging challenges

### Step 4: Generate Insights
- Connect performance to actions taken
- Predict future trends
- Suggest optimizations
- Highlight risks and opportunities

## Content Generation Guidelines

### Executive Summary
- Lead with the most important information
- Balance positive achievements with honest challenges
- Keep to 2-3 sentences maximum
- Focus on business impact

### Revenue Section
- Always show actual vs. target
- Include percentage of goal achieved
- Compare to previous periods
- Project monthly outcome

### KPI Section
- Use consistent formatting
- Highlight variances from target
- Show trend arrows (‚Üë‚Üì‚Üí) where appropriate
- Focus on actionable metrics

### Challenge Section
- Be specific about impacts
- Provide root cause analysis
- Show resolution status
- Be honest but constructive

## Analysis Depth

### Quantitative Analysis
- Revenue calculations and projections
- Performance metric comparisons
- Trend analysis and forecasting
- Efficiency measurements

### Qualitative Analysis
- Quality of customer interactions
- Effectiveness of strategies
- Team performance observations
- Market condition assessments

## Recommendation Framework

### Categorization
- **Immediate**: Actions needed this week
- **Short-term**: Changes for next 2-4 weeks
- **Medium-term**: Adjustments for next month
- **Strategic**: Long-term planning considerations

### Prioritization
- **Critical**: Business-threatening issues
- **High**: Revenue or efficiency impacts
- **Medium**: Process improvements
- **Low**: Nice-to-have optimizations

## Quality Standards

### Accuracy Requirements
- Verify all numerical data
- Cross-reference multiple sources
- Double-check calculations
- Ensure dates are correct

### Clarity Standards
- Use plain English
- Avoid jargon where possible
- Explain acronyms
- Maintain consistent terminology
